STOCK APPLICATION WEB-APP

LANGUAGES: Python, HTML, CSS 


FRAMEWORK: Django 



DATABASE: dbsqlite3


THIRD PARTY APIs: Braintree, Tiingo


Application is available at <"your ip">:9000 | localhost:9000


DESCRIPTION: 
A Stock Market webapp catering to registered users who have access to comprehensive information about publicly listed companies in the US stock market. The platform enables users to make deposits and purchase stocks seamlessly through the integration of third-party APIs, namely Tiingo for market data and Braintree for secure payment processing.
